System Logic & Other System Issues

This data element is required for all HMIS participating projects.

It is required for all heads of household and adult household members.' HMIS end users must be able to enter a response for household members who turn 18 while enrolled.

Regardless of when data are entered, responses must always reflect living situation and circumstances as of the Project Start Date.
